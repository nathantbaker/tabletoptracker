About The Rankings

Tabletop Tracker tracks which board games move the most in BoardGameGeek rankings. Rankings are based on the Geek Rating, which is the average of a game's user ratings, weighted for the number of it's ratings.

  • Chart data was last calculated . Data is calculated daily.
  • Tabletop Tracker is tracking all .
  • BoardGameGeek doesn't rank board games with less than 30 user ratings.
